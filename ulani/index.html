Background

ULANI is a newly created brand within the company, with the aim of promoting electronic paper and creating more products with electronic paper applications. The Eternal Calendar is the first product that was born, which is the world’s first electronic paper desk calendar. It provides a variety of theme options for users to freely choose their daily desk calendar theme, and it can synchronize with the user’s mobile calendar to display on the desk calendar. This replaces the traditional paper desk calendar with a more intelligent, convenient, and ritualistic alternative.

Product Story

Traditional desk calendars have evolved from paper to digital calendars on our smartphones. The e-paper desk calendar was created to offer a low-power alternative that takes advantage of the static nature of the display when not in use. The feature of flipping pages daily is perfect for a monthly calendar, and the product also includes various themes that can be switched daily to showcase one’s mood and enhance daily rituals. However, for users, the primary function of a calendar is to help them complete their daily events and schedule their time. The ability to sync with their phone’s schedule is essential. As a result, the product combines traditional features with digital capabilities, making it a unique and attractive option.
