Process Design

Design think

Through the research results, it helps me understand the current situation, find needs, assume opportunities, and confirm whether users have needs through qualitative or quantitative methods, so that design, business and technology can be combined into a good business model.

Requirements production function, fast iteration and sketching, through wireframe confirmation and process, help users complete goals and tasks, and improve experience and interaction through interface design.
